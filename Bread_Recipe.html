Wholemeal Bread Recipe

Here's a no-knead Wholemeal Bread Recipe to try:

INGREDIENTS

  • 400grams Bread Flour
  • 50 grams Wholemeal Flour
  • 500 grams Oats
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon oilve oil
  • 3 teaspoons yeast
  • 300 ml water



METHOD

  1. All all ingedients into a deep round countainer with a loose fitting lid
  2. Using your hand or a wooden spoon, slowly incorporate the ingredients, until it looks "shaggy" - it would be sticky and wet
  3. Try to shape it into a ball
  4. Cover loosely and leave for about 45-60 min in a place without any breeze (like a microwave oven, or cupboard) to rise till double its size or until it reaches the lid
  5. Put it in your fridge for 2 nights
  6. Take out the dough and let it come to room temperature (about 2 hours)
  7. Hold the dough with one hand, and grab some dough with the other hand. Stretch the dough in the bowl and fold it back. Pick up another corner and do the same, for about 8-10 times
  8. Shape it into a cylinrdical shape and put it into a bread loaf baking tin
  9. Cover with a damp cloth and let the dough rest in a covered place for about an hour until it rises and holds the shape of a bread loaf
  10. Bake in an oven at 220C for 40 minutes
  11. Once done, turn out the bread from the tin, and let it cool to room temperature
